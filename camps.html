  • Ages 6–10

    Vocal Performance Camp

    Vocal Performance Camp is a playful, encouraging camp for young singers who love music and want to build confidence using their voices. Campers will learn basic vocal technique, rhythm, pitch, breathing, expression, movement, microphone/stage confidence, and performance skills through age-appropriate songs and activities.

    Camp will conclude with a final showcase for families at the end of the week.

    Best for: kids who love to sing, perform, move, create, and build confidence in a fun, encouraging environment.

  • Ages 6–10

    Musical Theater Camp

    Musical Theater Camp is a fun, confidence-building performing arts camp for young singers, actors, and movers. Campers will explore singing, acting, movement, theater games, character work, and stage presence in a positive small-group environment.

    Camp will conclude with a final showcase for families at the end of the week.

    Best for: kids who love to sing, act, perform, pretend, move, or build confidence in front of others.

  • Ages 10–14

    Vocal Performance Workshop

    Vocal Performance Workshop is a small-group voice and performance intensive for students who want to become more confident, expressive singers. Students will work on healthy vocal technique, breath support, pitch, tone, song interpretation, stage presence, performance anxiety, microphone technique, and connecting emotionally to a song.

    Camp will conclude with a final showcase for families at the end of the week.

    Best for: students who want to strengthen their voice, understand the science of singing, build confidence, and train their voice like an instrument.

    Additional Workshop dates opening based on enrollment.

  • Ages 10–14

    Musical Theater Workshop

    Musical Theater Workshop is a small-group performance camp for students who want to grow as singers, actors, and storytellers. Campers will work on vocal performance, acting the song, character choices, movement, and stage presence.

    Camp will include a final showcase for families at the end of the week.

    Best for: students who enjoy musical theater, want more stage confidence, or are ready to strengthen their singing and performance skills.
