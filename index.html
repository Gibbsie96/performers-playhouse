Hi! I'm Nicole

I'm a music and theater instructor new to the Mesa area, and I'm so excited to bring what I love most, helping kids find their voice, to Eastmark this summer.

  • Professional Vocal Coach for 16+ years in Chicago
  • Students have gone on to Broadway, Disney & American Idol
  • Director of children's theater for 10+ years

What Campers Will Do

One-week workshops designed to grow confidence, creativity, and performance skills.

  • Voice Training

    Healthy singing technique, breath control, and finding their unique sound. The same fundamentals Nicole has taught for 16+ years.

  • Acting Classes

    Improv games, scene work, character building, and stage presence. Kids learn to express, listen, and react with confidence.

  • Musical Theater

    Putting it all together: songs, scenes, and choreography that come alive in a Friday showcase for family and friends.

Camp Details

  • WhenWeek-long workshops in June & July
  • WhereEastmark, Mesa, AZ
  • Ages6–10 and 10–14 (separate groups)
  • Group sizeSmall. Spots are intentionally limited, so every camper gets real coaching, not a crowd.
  • ShowcaseLive Friday performance for friends and family. A real stage, real applause, real "I did that" moments.
  • PaymentESA reimbursable, Classwallet vendor license in progress
